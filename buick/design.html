1986 Buick Regal:



Design Objectives

This project is all about creating a forever car.
I want to build, with my own hands, one car, to last me the rest of my life.

I chose this particular car to restore largely based on its platform: the 1980's GM "G-Body" Platform.
This means although the car is 35 years old, there are still lots of companies making brand new parts for the car, and a ton of information available online and in print about maintaining and repairng these cars.

The main artistic goal and vision with this project is to become proficient at sheetmetal welding and fabrication, with an art and design-oriented focus.

Completing this project provides ample opprtunity to gain substanstial skills in sheetmetal welding and fabrication.


Additional long term design goals include:

  • Custom Interior/Upholstering and working with interior fabrics
  • Custom Paint and working with spray guns and high end paints
  • EV Swap/Electric Conversion/Zero Emissions Drivetrain
In the future, I'll use this space to organize my ideas and desing objectives as I cross the "halfway mark" with this project this summer.
