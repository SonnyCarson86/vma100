I'd like to introduce you to my current design project/obsession:

My 1986 Buick Regal.



I bought the car in 2012 from the brother of one of my co-workers; it originally belonged to their uncle. It had been in the family since being purchased brand new in 1986. I paid $750 for it. The vehicle came with all the original paperwork, including the bill of sale, receipt, service records and even the original dealership window sticker, dispalying all the original options the buyer chose when he ordered the car from the showroom floor.

After being my "summer car" for 5+ years, I started a full body frame off restoration in the summer of 2018. What I've got to show you is the result of 4 summers of hard work dedication and e x t r e m e budgeting.

Thanks for looking, and wish me luck, because I have no idea what I'm doing, I'm just learning as I go along!
